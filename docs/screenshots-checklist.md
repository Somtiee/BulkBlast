# Screenshots Checklist

Capture these specific screens for the dApp Store listing. Ensure the status bar is clean (full battery, no notifications) or removed.

## 1. Create / Home Screen
*   **Context**: The main landing page.
*   **Focus**: Show the clean "Create Drop" interface, token selection, and "Wallet / Bulk Blast" toggle.
*   **Caption**: "Start an Airdrop in Seconds"

## 2. Wallet Portfolio
*   **Context**: The Wallet tab or asset selection modal.
*   **Focus**: Show a list of tokens (SOL, USDC, SKR) with balances.
*   **Caption**: "Manage Your Solana Assets"

## 3. Bulk Send Setup (Recipients)
*   **Context**: The "Add Recipients" or "Scan/Upload" screen.
*   **Focus**: Show a list of recipients (e.g., "5 Recipients added") with amounts.
*   **Caption**: "Import Lists via CSV or Text"

## 4. Review & Safety Check
*   **Context**: The Review screen before execution.
*   **Focus**: Show the summary card, total cost, and the "Safety Check Passed" badge.
*   **Caption**: "Built-in Safety Simulations"

## 5. Execution Progress
*   **Context**: The screen shown *during* a drop.
*   **Focus**: Progress bar filling up, transaction hashes appearing.
*   **Caption**: "Fast & Reliable Execution"

## 6. Built-in Swap
*   **Context**: The Jupiter Swap modal.
*   **Focus**: Swapping SOL for another token (e.g., "You Pay 1 SOL -> You Receive 340 SKR").
*   **Caption**: "Powered by Jupiter Aggregator"

## 7. History & Receipts
*   **Context**: The History tab.
*   **Focus**: List of past drops with "Success" status and the Export CSV button.
*   **Caption**: "Track & Export Your History"

## 8. Settings / Dark Mode
*   **Context**: The Settings screen (showing Dark Mode toggle or Network switch).
*   **Focus**: Clean typography and organization.
*   **Caption**: "Customizable Experience"
