# Polished Demo Sequence

Follow this exact flow when recording the demo video or presenting live.

## 1. The "Hook" (0:00 - 0:10)
- **Action**: Launch the app from the home screen.
- **Visual**: Splash screen animation -> Smooth transition to the "Create Drop" screen.
- **Narrative**: "This is BulkBlast, the fastest way to send assets on Solana."

## 2. Portfolio & Setup (0:10 - 0:30)
- **Action**: Tap the "Wallet" toggle.
- **Visual**: Show balances loading instantly. Scroll down to see tokens.
- **Action**: Tap "Create Drop" toggle back.
- **Action**: Tap token selector (SOL dropdown). Select a token (e.g., USDC or SKR).
- **Narrative**: "Connect your wallet, view your portfolio, and select any asset to send."

## 3. The Bulk Import (0:30 - 0:50)
- **Action**: Tap "Add Recipients".
- **Action**: Tap "CSV Upload" (or "Scan" if live).
- **Visual**: File picker opens -> Select `airdrop_list.csv`.
- **Visual**: Screen populates with 50+ recipients instantly.
- **Narrative**: "Import hundreds of addresses from a CSV in seconds. No manual entry required."

## 4. Safety & Review (0:50 - 1:10)
- **Action**: Tap "Next".
- **Visual**: "Running Safety Check..." spinner -> "Safety Check Passed" badge.
- **Visual**: Show the cost summary card.
- **Narrative**: "Our built-in simulation engine ensures your transaction will succeed before you pay a cent."

## 5. Execution (1:10 - 1:30)
- **Action**: Tap "Execute Airdrop".
- **Visual**: Progress bar fills up. Transaction hashes appear one by one.
- **Visual**: "Success!" screen.
- **Narrative**: "Blast off. Assets are distributed in a single atomic flow."

## 6. History & Export (1:30 - 1:45)
- **Action**: Tap "View Receipt" (or go to History tab).
- **Visual**: Tap the top transaction. Show the details.
- **Action**: Tap "Export CSV". Show the share sheet.
- **Narrative**: "Keep compliant with instant exportable receipts."

## 7. The Swap (Optional/Bonus) (1:45 - 2:00)
- **Action**: Go to Home -> Tap Swap icon.
- **Visual**: Enter amount -> See Jupiter quote load.
- **Narrative**: "Need more tokens? Swap instantly with our Jupiter integration."
