Roadmap

Our plan to build the premier mobile bulk transaction tool on Solana.

Q1 2026 - Launch & Core Features

Mobile-First Launch

  • Initial Android Release (APK)
  • Core Bulk Send Engine (SOL + SPL Tokens)
  • Basic NFT Support (Metaplex Standard)
  • Fee Engine with SKR and SOL payment modes
  • Safety Rails (Simulation, Spend Caps)
  • Solana Mobile Discount Integration
  • BAGS Launch & Blast workflow
  • DFLOW routing support alongside Jupiter
Q2 2026 - Enhanced Utility

Advanced Features & Routing

  • Expanded NFT Handling (Collections, larger batches)
  • Advanced Swap Routing refinements (Jupiter + DFLOW)
  • Improved Wallet Asset Intelligence
  • Performance & UX Refinements
  • Comprehensive Analytics Dashboard
Q3 2026 - Ecosystem Expansion

Community & Integrations

  • Broader Ecosystem Partner Integrations
  • Advanced Recipient Workflows (Tagging, Filters)
  • Treasury & Team Operations Tooling
  • Campaign Management Features for Community Managers
Q4 2026 - Enterprise & Beyond

Scale & Enterprise

  • Team / Multi-Sig Wallet Support (Squads Integration)
  • Enterprise-Grade Reporting & Exports
  • Expanded Asset Tooling (cNFTs, Token Extensions)
  • iOS Beta (Subject to App Store Policies)

Community Driven

This roadmap is flexible and driven by community feedback. Have a feature request? Let us know on Twitter or GitHub.
