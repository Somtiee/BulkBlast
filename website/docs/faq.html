Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about BulkBlast functionality, launch flow, safety, and fees.

What assets can I send with BulkBlast?

BulkBlast supports the native Solana token (SOL), all standard SPL tokens (like USDC, BONK, WIF), and Metaplex standard NFTs.

Is BulkBlast mobile-only?

Yes, BulkBlast is designed as a mobile-first application specifically for Android. We believe the future of crypto adoption lies in powerful mobile tools. An iOS version is planned for the future.

What networks are supported?

BulkBlast supports both Solana Mainnet-Beta (for real transactions) and Devnet (for testing). You can switch networks easily in the Settings tab.

Can I pay service fees in SOL or SKR?

Yes. BulkBlast supports both fee modes. You can pay in SKR or SOL at checkout, and the app shows the exact amount before you confirm.

How does the Solana Mobile discount work?

If you are using a Solana Mobile device (Saga or Seeker), the app automatically detects this and applies a 50% discount to BulkBlast service fees in both SKR and SOL modes.

What is Launch & Blast with BAGS?

You can launch a token via BAGS in-app, then tap BLAST NOW to move directly into bulk distribution with the new token pre-filled.

Do launch users get a free blast?

There is a one-time promo path: if you launch and immediately hit BLAST NOW, that next blast can be fee-free once. If you skip the handoff, the promo is not retained.

Is BulkBlast safe to use?

Yes. BulkBlast is a non-custodial tool, meaning we never have access to your private keys or funds. All transactions are simulated before signing to ensure safety. We recommend testing on Devnet first if you are unsure.

Are swaps done inside the app?

Yes. BulkBlast integrates Jupiter and supports DFLOW routing paths, allowing you to swap assets without leaving the app.

Are partner/API keys exposed in the APK?

No. Sensitive third-party keys are held server-side in the proxy. The app calls proxy endpoints and does not bundle private partner API keys in the client.

Is documentation still evolving?

Yes, as BulkBlast adds new features (like improved NFT support, new routing partners, etc.), this documentation will be updated. Check back often for the latest guides.
