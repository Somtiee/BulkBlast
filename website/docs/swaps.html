Swaps (Jupiter)

Convert assets instantly without leaving the app, powered by Jupiter Aggregator.

Why In-App Swaps?

BulkBlast integrates Jupiter, Solana's leading swap aggregator, directly into the mobile experience. This allows users to:

  • Pay Fees Instantly: Convert SOL to SEEKER (SKR) for transaction fees without needing an external exchange.
  • Reduce Friction: Complete your entire bulk send workflow within a single app.
  • Best Execution: Get the best rates across all Solana DEXs.

How to Swap

  1. Navigate to Swap Tap the Swap icon in the bottom navigation bar.
  2. Select Assets Choose the token you want to sell (e.g., SOL) and the token you want to buy (e.g., SKR).
  3. Enter Amount Input the amount you wish to swap.
  4. Review Quote Jupiter will find the best route. Review the rate, slippage, and fees.
  5. Confirm Swipe to confirm the swap. The assets will be available in your wallet instantly.

Future Integrations

While Jupiter powers our current swap infrastructure, BulkBlast is actively exploring additional routing partners to ensure redundancy and optimal performance:

  • dFlow: Advanced order flow routing for potentially better execution.
  • Additional Aggregators: Expanding our DEX support for niche tokens.

These integrations are part of our roadmap to make BulkBlast the most robust mobile transaction tool on Solana.

Safety First

All swaps are executed on-chain via Jupiter's secure smart contracts. BulkBlast does not hold or custody your assets during the swap process.
