Fees & Payments

Understand BulkBlast's transparent fee structure, with payment options in both SKR and SOL.

Solana Mobile Discount

Exclusive Benefit: Solana Mobile (Saga / Seeker) users automatically receive a 50% discount on all BulkBlast service fees (SKR and SOL modes).

Fee Tiers

BulkBlast charges a small service fee to support ongoing development and infrastructure. Fees are calculated based on the number of recipients in your bulk transaction.

Recipients Base Fee (SKR mode, USD value) Base Fee (SOL mode, USD value) Solana Mobile (50% OFF)
1 - 49 $0.05 $0.065 $0.025 / $0.0325
50 - 499 $0.10 $0.14 $0.05 / $0.07
500+ $0.20 $0.30 $0.10 / $0.15

*These are USD-denominated service fees. At checkout, BulkBlast calculates token amount from live price and lets you pay in SKR or SOL.

Why offer both SKR and SOL?

BulkBlast supports both options so users can execute quickly without leaving flow. This allows us to:

  • Offer default SKR-aligned pricing for Solana Mobile users.
  • Let users pay in SOL when SKR balance is unavailable.
  • Keep fees transparent via USD-based tiers.

Launch & Blast Promo

If a user launches through BAGS and immediately taps BLAST NOW, that next blast can be fee-free once. If the user skips the BLAST action, promo eligibility is not retained.

Treasury & Transparency

All collected fees are routed directly to the BulkBlast treasury wallet. These funds are used strictly for:

  • Product development (new features, iOS support).
  • Infrastructure costs (RPC nodes, hosting).
  • Community initiatives and ecosystem growth.

We believe in full transparency. You will always see the exact fee amount before confirming any transaction.

Devnet Fees

On Devnet, fees may be waived or use "fake" SKR for testing purposes. Always verify the fee amount on the confirmation screen.
