Fees & Payments

Understand BulkBlast's transparent fee structure, powered by SEEKER (SKR) tokens.

Solana Mobile Discount

Exclusive Benefit: Solana Mobile (Saga / Seeker) users automatically receive a 50% discount on all BulkBlast fees.

Fee Tiers

BulkBlast charges a small service fee to support ongoing development and infrastructure. Fees are calculated based on the number of recipients in your bulk transaction.

Recipients Fee (in SKR) Solana Mobile Fee
1 - 49 $0.50 equivalent $0.25 equivalent
50 - 499 $1.00 equivalent $0.50 equivalent
500+ $2.00 equivalent $1.00 equivalent

*Fees are payable in SEEKER (SKR) tokens. The USD equivalent is calculated at the time of transaction.

Why SEEKER (SKR)?

BulkBlast utilizes the SEEKER (SKR) token for fee payments to align with the Solana Mobile ecosystem. This allows us to:

  • Offer native discounts to mobile users.
  • Streamline fee processing.
  • Support the broader Solana community.

Treasury & Transparency

All collected fees are routed directly to the BulkBlast treasury wallet. These funds are used strictly for:

  • Product development (new features, iOS support).
  • Infrastructure costs (RPC nodes, hosting).
  • Community initiatives and ecosystem growth.

We believe in full transparency. You will always see the exact fee amount before confirming any transaction.

Devnet Fees

On Devnet, fees may be waived or use "fake" SKR for testing purposes. Always verify the fee amount on the confirmation screen.
