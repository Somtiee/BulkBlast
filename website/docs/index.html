Solana Mobile Hackathon Project

BulkBlast is built for the MONOLITH Solana Mobile Hackathon, designed as a real, mobile-first product for the Solana ecosystem.

What is BulkBlast?

BulkBlast is a mobile-first Solana transaction tool that lets users launch tokens and send SOL, SPL tokens, or NFTs to many wallets from Android. It replaces complex desktop scripts and spreadsheet chaos with a clean, safe workflow.

Optimized for community managers, token teams, and power users, BulkBlast brings full "launch and distribute" capability to your phone with built-in safety checks, transparent fees, and integrated routing.

Who is this for?

Token Teams

Distribute tokens to early supporters, team members, or contest winners instantly.

NFT Projects

Send WL tokens, rewards, or commemorative NFTs to your community from anywhere.

DAO Managers

Execute payroll, grant distributions, or operational payments without a laptop.

Power Users

Manage multiple wallets and complex asset transfers with professional tools.

Why BulkBlast?

  • Mobile-First: Designed natively for Android and Solana Mobile devices.
  • Safer Sending: Simulations, spend caps, and confirmation safeguards prevent mistakes.
  • Full Asset Support: Works with SOL, SPL Tokens, and Metaplex NFTs.
  • BAGS Launch Flow: Launch tokens in-app and jump straight into distribution.
  • Flexible Fees: Pay service fees in either SKR or SOL, with live conversion in-app.
  • Integrated Routing: Jupiter + DFLOW integrations help keep execution efficient.
  • Speed: Import recipients from CSV or scan QR codes in seconds.
